Commissioning New Works

Central to the Kaibab Trio’s mission is commissioning new music for oboe, clarinet, and piano — expanding the repertoire and amplifying historically underrepresented voices. We are especially drawn to works inspired by the wildlife and scenery of northern Arizona.

Composers and presenters interested in a commission or consortium are warmly invited to get in touch.
